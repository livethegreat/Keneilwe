SURVIVING

Healing through connection

Adversity comes in many forms, sometimes it is hard to interpret our emotions, which makes it harder for us to learn from them, and because of this we feel alone. The best way to move past individual trauma is through connection and community-healing ourselves and one another.

UNLOCKING POTENTIAL

Empowering Education

In my 30 years of teaching experience, I have been providing practical solutions and insights on wellness topics to help educators and students achieve their full potential.

Unlock your emotional freedom

This book will help you if:

  • You are having a difficult time processing your feelings, which can quickly turn into reactions against yourself and others.

  • You have feelings of worthlessness or guilt, fixating on past failures or self-blame.

  • You have an overwhelming feeling of isolation regardless of where you are and who’s around, or when you try to connect or reach out to others, it's not reciprocated.

  • You are frustrated by the lack of progress you’re making at work and questioning whether you’re on the right path professionally.

  • You are grieving a loss of a loved one.

  • You have trouble sleeping, unable to turn your thoughts off. You are struggling to focus and you are easily destructed by stimuli.

Who am I?

My name is Keneilwe Tsieane, I am a highly accomplished and experienced educator with over 30 years of teaching experience. I’ve been confronted with a lot of the same stressful and demanding situations that you’re experiencing today. I understand what it takes to stay confident and decisive, even after setbacks or hurdles that get in the way.

In my book, I share stories of people I've helped, and actionable insights to help you on your healing journey, along with reflections from my personal experiences.

I wrote this book to help you finally face your struggles, get unstuck, and transform your thinking-to claim agency in your own life and circumstances, and to use this power to help heal even those around you.
