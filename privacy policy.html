Pricacy Policy

Last updated October 02, 2023.

Keneilwe Tsieane is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information when you visit our website keneilwetsieane.com and purchase our self-help book, "My Walk with God".

By using our website and purchasing the book, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our website or purchase the book.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you use our website or purchase the book:

1. Contact Information: We may collect your name, email address, and postal address when you purchase the book.

2. Payment Information: We may collect payment information, including credit card details, when you make a purchase.

3. Website Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

1. To process and fulfill your purchase of the book.

2. To communicate with you about your purchase and provide customer support.

3. To send you promotional emails and updates about our products and services, but only if you have opted in to receive such communications.

4. To improve our website and services based on your feedback and usage.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to third parties under the following circumstances:

1. To trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and fulfilling your purchase.

2. To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal processes.

3. To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our customers and the public.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

1. You may update or correct your personal information by contacting us at keneilwe.kt@gmail.com.

2. You may opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions included in those emails.

Children's Privacy

Our website and book are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and any changes will be posted on this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at keneilwe.kt@gmail.com.
